Variation

In order to facilitate immediate analysis of the 1000 Genomes Project data by the whole scientific community, this browser (based on Ensembl) integrates the SNP and INDEL calls from the phase 1 integrated release.

This data has be submitted to dbSNP, and once rsid's have been allocated, will be absorbed into the UCSC and Ensembl browsers according to their respective release cycles. Until that point any non rs SNP id's on this site are temporary and will NOT be maintained.
